Teacher Center Plan and Present
Teacher Center Plan and Present
Lesson Planning tools allow you to add specific lessons to your teaching calendar, customize & create lesson plans for the chapter and lessons, and print lessons to PDF for offline learning.
The Presentation button accesses presentations that walk you through the lesson
The lesson’s objectives and alignment with academic concepts, skills, and standards are displayed under the Focus folder. 
Lesson-reinforcing resources are available on the right side of the screen.
Click the arrow next to Focus to close the Focus folder.
All the folders in this lesson are now displayed. Click the arrow next to Teach.
The Teach folder displays lesson strategies, exercises, activities, and resources.
Click a resource’s Gear Icon to display a menu of options.
Click Assign this Resource.
Teacher Center Assign
The Assignment Name and Instructions field allows you to name an assignment, provide instructions for the assignment, identify specific students that need to complete the assignment, and choose when an assignment will expire.
The Make an Assignment screen displays. The resource being assigned is located on the right-hand side of the page in the Attach Resources and Content field.
To add an additional resource to a homework assignment, click Edit in the Attach Resources and Content field.
Use this field to select students who will receive assignments and to also send out student assignment notifications.
Teacher Center Assign
The Edit Resources and Content screen displays. Click the Add button under a resource to add it to the assignment. In this example, we are clicking Add to attach LearnSmart®, a practice and study tool, to the assignment.
When you are finished adding assignment resources, click Done.
Teacher Center Resources
Click Resources to open the Resources screen and view resources organized by chapter and lesson.
Click Keyword Search to search for resources. Click the star under a resource to add it to the Favorites tab that is adjacent to the Keyword Search tab. 
Teacher Center Resources

ConnectED Tech Tips
Before you start your digital review, there are a few things to take care of:
• Disable pop-up blockers; some pieces of interactivity will open in a new window • Un-mute your device • If possible, use a hard-wired connection to the internet • Close unnecessary applications (they can slow down your machine) • This review site is not mobile-friendly; please view on a laptop or desktop
